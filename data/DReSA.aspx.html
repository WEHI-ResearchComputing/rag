Digital Research Skills Australasia (DReSA)

Calling all researchers at WEHI...

If you are not yet familiar with the DReSA, we highly recommend you pay a visit to their website.

The DReSA has received investments from the Pawsey Supercomputing Research Centre and the Australian Research Data Commons (ARDC) and its creators seek to:

  • Lift Australasian researchers' capabilities and skills by improving the findability of events and resources
  • Build national, collaborative, training partnerships
  • Strategically leverage trainers' and learners' time and resources

DReSA makes it easier for learners, trainers and training providers to find digital research skills-focused educational events and resources. Visit their website and subscribe to stay informed of their upcoming research training events, or make use of their high-quality research training material and resources.

    You will find research training courses and materials from content providers that include:

    • AARnet
    • AeRo
    • Australian BioCommons
    • Australian Research Data Commons (ARDC)
    • Australian Space Data Analysis Facility
    • Federation University
    • Intersect Australia
    • Monash University
    • NCI Australia
    • Pawsey Supercomputing Research Centre
    • QCIF
    • Research Software Engineering Australasia (RSE)
    • The University of Sydney

    All resources on the site are free of charge and easy to access.

    DReSA is an excellent source to learn about upcoming research training events and the site, as well as its content providers list, is growing on a regular basis.

    Visit DReSA at dresa.org.au/.
