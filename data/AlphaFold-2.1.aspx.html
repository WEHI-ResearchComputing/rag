DeepMind recently shared AlphaFold-Multimer, a model that can predict the structure of multi-chain protein complexes with increased accuracy. The source code to account for multi-chain protein complexes, which will provide a significant improvement in accuracy for predicting protein interactions is available also dpmd.ai/af-multimer-os 

You can also generate predictions from your browser via: dpmd.ai/alphafold-colab. And soon AlphaFold 2.1 will be available on Milton.
