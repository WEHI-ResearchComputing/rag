Grant text

To include in grants when describing available resources and support. Below are two examples:

WEHI Research Computing provides the necessary research computing infrastructure including a >5000-core computer cluster, >60TB memory, GPU farm, >10 petabytes of tiered-storage, and highly-skilled engineering support through our Research Computing Platform.

WEHI Research Computing operates a robust high-performance computing cluster (HPC) equipped with over 5000 cores, more than 60TB of memory, a 58 GPU farm that includes A30s, A100s, A10s and an overall memory of more than 7TB. The HPC has an upwards of 10 petabytes of tiered storage. The HPC system employs Slurm for efficient cluster management and job scheduling.

Administration of the HPC platform falls under the purview of the Research Computing teams, which is composed of two teams. The ITS Research Systems (ITS RS) team, comprised of 5 FTE, is responsible for maintaining the infrastructure and overseeing the HPC environment. Meanwhile, the Research Computing Platform team, consisting of 5 FTE, provides software engineering support and collaborates closely with researchers to develop pipelines and research systems.

Acknowledgements

Acknowledgements are not mandatory, but are encouraged for the purposes of metric gathering, as well as to show appreciation for members of Research Computing who provide their skills and services to support your research.

Acknowledging use of Milton Compute Resources only

Recommended if any of the research is performed using any of Milton's compute resources

This research was undertaken with the assistance of Milton HPC/Virtual Machines, supported by WEHI.

E.g., This research was undertaken with the assistance of Milton Virtual Machines, supported by WEHI.

Acknowledging RCP

If multiple members of RCP provided support e.g., debugging or advice, we recommend including RCP in your acknowledgments.

I/We thank WEHI's Research Computing Platform for <contribution>

e.g.

  • I thank WEHI's Research Computing Platform for advice in developing my pipeline

Acknowledging RCP members

If you would like specifically acknowledge a member of RCP for their help:

I/We thank <firstname> <lastname> from WEHI's Research Computing Platform for <contribution>

e.g.

  • We thank Michael Milton from WEHI's Research Computing Platform for recommendations for our database architecture.
  • We thank Edward Yang from WEHI's Research Computing Platform for running our analysis pipeline on WEHI's Milton HPC.
  • We thank Julie Iskander from WEHI's Research Computing Platform for advice on using Alphafold in our pipeline.

Authorship

If you are approaching RCP with work that might lead to publication, please discuss authorship early!
