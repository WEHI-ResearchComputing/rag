Milton 2022 uplift - complete this survey now to tell us the changes you want & need

It is time for the 2022 Milton uplift. Milton is regularly updated to meet researcher requirements. Research Computing Teams (which spans ITS Research Systems and the Research Computing Platform in Computational Biology Theme) are planning the next capital expenditure for Milton. The 2021 uplift focused on GPU nodes. ​​​​​​​

Please complete this short survey (approximately 3 mins to complete) and let us know what changes you need, or would like, to see that will enable you to use Milton more efficiently.

Please feel free to share the survey link with as many Milton and SLURM users as possible. The more people that respond to the survey, the better understanding we will have of our users' needs and wants. This, in turn, will aid us to produce the best possible upgrade for everyone.

The survey will shut at COB on Monday 2 May 2022.

If you have any questions about the survey or the upgrade, please contact research.computing@wehi.edu.au.
