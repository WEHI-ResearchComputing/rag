This article will discuss public data hosting for research data. One notable reason for finding such hosting is due to journal requirements. For example, Oxford University Press requires its authors to publicly release all data underlying a publication. However, WEHI does not currently provide a mechanism for hosting such data, so you may need to investigate external services, both free and paid, for this purpose.
