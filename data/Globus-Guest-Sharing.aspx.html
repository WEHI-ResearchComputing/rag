We’re excited to announce that the Globus platform (accessible at app.globus.org) can now be used to share WEHI data with external collaborators! Globus is a data movement and transfer platform that has been in place at WEHI for several months, but we hope that this new feature will prove useful for many of your workloads.

Full information is available under the ‘Guest Sharing’ section of Globus guide, but the key points are:

  • Only one-way sharing is available (from WEHI to externals, not external to WEHI)
  • Data must be copied or moved to your VAST Scratch area prior to sharing (as sharing is only available from VAST Scratch)
  • Domains of recipients must be pre-approved by our security team (though we’ll be adding to this list continually as researchers request new domains be added!)

If you’re keen to get started, you’ll need to submit a one-off support ticket to gain access to sharing functionality (note that basic non-sharing functionality is already available to all users) - just email us at support@wehi.edu.au, and ask to be added to the Globus Sharing group. We’ll require a brief justification, approval from your lab head, and sign-off on our T&Cs
