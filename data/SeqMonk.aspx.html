SeqMonk

SeqMonk is a program that enables the visualisation and analysis of mapped sequence data. SeqMonk is developed by Babraham Bioinformatics.


Accessing SeqMonk Via Open OnDemand Portal

Go to Open OnDemand and choose SeqMonk

How to change SeqMonk preferences?
​​​​​​​

  • Edit > Preferences

Accessing SeqMonk VM

SeqMonk VMs has been moved to Open OnDemand. It cannot be provisioned through the VM catalog anymore.


Accessing SeqMonk through vc7-shared or SLURM

SeqMonk can be run through the command-line on vc7-shared.

ssh -Y vc7-shared\n\nmodule av seqmonk\n\n----------------- /stornext/System/data/modulefiles/bioinf/its -----------------\nseqmonk/1.45.1          seqmonk/1.47.1          seqmonk/1.48.1(default)\nseqmonk/1.46            seqmonk/1.47.2\nseqmonk/1.47.0          seqmonk/1.48.0\n\n\nmodule load seqmonk\nseqmonk
