RCP training for Milton users - the 2022 survey

Research Computing is aware that our users have multiple and varied training requirements that need to be fulfilled.

With this in mind, we have created a short survey to find out what your personal requirements are for 2022. The survey will take 1 minute to complete and your feedback is essential for us to create a learning schedule for 2022 that will fulfill as many users' requirements as possible.

Please talk about this survey with your colleagues and share the link. The more people that tell us what they want to learn, the better the learning schedule will be.
