Stay connected

This website serves as a valuable resource while using Milton, but there are additional support channels available outside of the website to enhance your experience with Milton.

We recommend staying connected to Milton news and engaging with other Milton users by joining our Slack channel and the Milton User Group (MUG) mailing list.

The milton-wehi slack channel allows you to interact with other users, discuss questions, share ideas, and stay informed about what other Milton users are working on.

Join the Milton User Group (MUG) mailing list (mug@wehi.edu.au) here. Once you're added to the MUG, you'll receive email updates about important research computing news as it happens.

If you are in the planning stage of developing or moving a workload to the HPC, or have open-ended issues beyond the scope of the Helpdesk, please contact Research Computing Platform (RCP) by emailing research.computing@wehi.edu.au.

We also welcome any feedback or suggestions for additional information to be included on this website. Your input is vital to ensuring that the site remains relevant, up-to-date, and helpful for your needs.

Please make use of these resources and communication channels to optimize your experience with Milton and our research computing services.
