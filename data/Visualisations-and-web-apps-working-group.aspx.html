​​​​​​​Visualisations and web apps working group

The ability to publish small web apps and visualisations (like R/Shiny apps) is not fully supported at WEHI either in terms of policy or infrastructure. A few weeks ago, we sent out a survey to get a broad understanding of needs and to solicit volunteers to join a working group. A summary of the results is here.

Researchers would like to publish a variety of apps from interactive visualisations, analysis tools, and reports. They need to reach a variety of audiences ranging from the general research community to access controlled collaborators. While R/Shiny is a popular technology, there are (too) many others. 

A number of people expressed interest in joining a working group and we are in the process of sending out invitations. The first task of the group will be to develop a broad understanding of requirements and then dig into what might reasonably be delivered.

The Research Data Governance Steering Committee (RDGSC)

There is a newly formed steering committee called the Research Data Governance Steering Committee (RDGSC), which is there to provide leadership, direction, and oversight to the planning and management of research data governance, and also to ensure ongoing alignment with the institute’s objectives by delivering value and efficiency.

The RDGSC is due to meet for the first time in July.

If you have any questions or comments about the RDGSC, please contact Rowland Mosbergen (mosbergen.r@wehi.edu.au).
