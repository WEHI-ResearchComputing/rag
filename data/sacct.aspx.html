sacct

The sacct command obtains detailed accounting information about jobs:

sacct -j <jobid>


To show information about all completed, cancelled or failed jobs from a specific start date and for a specific user, type:

sacct -S <startdate> -u <username>


Your results will look similar to this example below:

$ sacct -S 2020-01-01 -u james.s\n\nJobID    JobName       Partition  Account  AllocCPUS  State     ExitCode\n------- ---------------------- ---------- ---------- ---------- --------\n10243       test-job   skylakeq               1     CANCELLED+      0:0\n10281       myjobtest  skylakeq               2     CANCELLED+      0:0\n10282       myjobtest  broadwellq             2     COMPLETED       0:0\n10282.batch batch                             2     COMPLETED       0:0\n10283       noemail    broadwellq             2     COMPLETED       0:0\n


To see information about YOUR jobs that have completed, cancelled or failed, type the following, without spaces:

sacct --format=User,JobID,Jobname,partition,state,time,start,end,elapsed,MaxRss,MaxVMSize,nnodes,ncpus,nodelist


Your results will look similar to the example shown below:

$ sacct --format=User,JobID,Jobname,partition,state,time,start,end,elapsed,MaxRss,MaxVMSize,nnodes,ncpus,nodelist\n\nUser     JobID    JobName  Partition  State          Timelimit               Start                 End           Elapsed    MaxRSS  MaxVMSize  NNodes  NCPUS   NodeList\n--------- ------------ ---------- ---------- ---------- ---------- ------------------- ------------------- ---------- ---------- ---------- -------- ---------- -------------------------------------------------\njames.s 10367  emailtest    gpuq  COMPLETED   00:05:00 2020-02-13T10:14:29 2020-02-13T10:14:40   00:00:11                                      1          2   SLURM-gpu-n01\n         10367.batch   batch             COMPLETED                  2020-02-13T10:14:29 2020-02-13T10:14:40   00:00:11      1196K    273616K   1          2   SLURM-gpu-n01\njames.s 10368  emailtest2  gpuq  COMPLETED   00:05:00 2020-02-13T12:32:59 2020-02-13T12:33:10   00:00:11                                      1          2   SLURM-gpu-n01\n         10368.batch    batch             COMPLETED                2020-02-13T12:32:59 2020-02-13T12:33:10   00:00:11      1312K    273616K    1         2   SLURM-gpu-n01\n


To see information about completed, cancelled &/or failed jobs from a specific user on a specific start date, type the following:

sacct --starttime <yyyy-mm-dd> -u <username> --format=User,JobID,Jobname,partition,state,time,start,end,elapsed,MaxRss,MaxVMSize,nnodes,ncpus,nodelist
