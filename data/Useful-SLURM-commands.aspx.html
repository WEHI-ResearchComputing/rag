SLURM command                                
                                               

Description

sinfoView information about nodes and partitions
sbatchSubmit a job (see our job scripts guide for more information)
sallocSubmit interactive job or a job step (read more in our Interactive workloads guide)
srunSubmit an interactive job or a job step, which is not covered in this guide (read more in our Interactive workloads guide)
scancelCancel a job
squeueView jobs located in the Slurm scheduling queue
scontrolView Slurm configuration and state
sacctGet historical statistics on jobs
sstatGet live statistics on running jobs


Tip:

  • man <command> for an explanation of the command, along with a list of any sub-commands that can be used with it
  • <command> --help for a brief summary of options that can be used in conjunction with your command. All commands are case sensitive so watch out and make sure you use the correct case when typing your commands.
