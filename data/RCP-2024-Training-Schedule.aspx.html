Research Computing Platform - 2024 Workshops 

The Research Computing Team is offering a series of workshops in 2024 to help develop your coding skills.

 

This page provides an up to date schedule of our planned workshops. Add these to your calendar and subscribe to the MUG mailing list for updates and registration information.

 

If you have any questions about RCP training you can contact us at: research.computing@wehi.edu.au

 

Registration for these workshops will be available through the Development and Learning page.
