scancel

The scancel command is used to cancel a pending or running job or a job step.

scancel <jobid>\n


In the example below we also used %9r %19S to learn the START TIME and REASON the jobs listed are in their current state:

squeue --format="%.6i %.10P %.8u %15a %.15j %.3t %9r %19S %.10M %.10L %.5D %.4C %Q %N" -u james.s
