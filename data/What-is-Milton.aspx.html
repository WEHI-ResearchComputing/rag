Who, or what, is Milton?


If you are new to WEHI, or simply new to using the research computing facilities at WEHI, you may have heard colleagues talk about using Milton.

Milton is the Research Computing mascot and is visually represented by the blue monster that you see on this page banner and the right-side of this column.

Milton represents our research computing high-performance computer (HPC) facilities and therefore using Milton simply means using WEHI's HPC.

To learn how to access and use Milton, have a look at our Using Milton Page.
