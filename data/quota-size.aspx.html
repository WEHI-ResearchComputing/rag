What size is my quota?

And how much space is left?..

  • Initial quotas are set at usage plus 25% (as of 01/12/2017), or 20Gb, whichever is greater.
  • Quotas are only calculated from data on disk; truncated data does not contribute to quotas.
  • The quota should be large enough to support your research data storage needs on the Home filesystem at current rates.​​
  • If you already know you​ require an increase to your quota, please submit a request to the ITS Helpdesk stating the reason. Each request will be considered on a case-by-case basis.​​
  • To find out both your quota size and current usage, log in to Institute Account Management​
