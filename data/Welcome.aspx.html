WEHI Research Computing (RC) is made up of two teams: IT Research Systems (ITS RS) and Research Computing Platform (RCP). Contact information for these teams can be found  here. 

The Research Computing (RC) teams at WEHI are dedicated to providing researchers with the necessary computational and human resources to successfully advance their research projects. We are committed to supporting your scientific research by offering fast and efficient methods for conducting reproducible, computational, and data-intensive research. Our mission is to enhance research efficiency through compute, data management, community engagement, and consultancy support. We aim to develop an institute-wide capability that enables data to be safely integrated, shared, and published, in compliance with grant, publication, and regulatory standards.

Our primary hardware resource is an on-premise HPC called Milton, specifically designed to cater to the complex needs of researchers and their laboratories. Milton is a high-powered computing system capable of handling the intricate tasks and large-sized files that conventional computers struggle with. Built with research in mind, it also adheres to policies and procedures that ensure compliance with legal requirements related to research data. 

Compute Resources 

One of the main components of Milton is a SLURM batch system and there are several options for interactive work as well. Milton hardware configuration page and SLURM partitions  page will take you through the available resources, limitations and uses. We have an extensive library of scientific software and will help you get access to any other software that you need through the modules system.  This overview will get you started with the documentation. We have a Milton User Group (MUG) mailing list, our main communication channel with all Milton users. Subscribe and follow it so that you are kept up-to-date with our monthly newsletter, the latest research computing news, and important system events. We also have dashboards to monitor resource utilisation. They are accessible from within WEHI’s network only. 

As collaborations, digital data, computational methods, and research outputs continue to expand, computing becomes increasingly essential. As data grows, so does the effort required to manage, explore, analyze, and share it. Research Computing provides platforms and expertise to help researchers scale their work beyond the desktop. Our RC teams can also help researchers access public clouds (such as AWS, Azure, or Google), tier 1 facilities (e.g., Pawsey, NCI), or facilities at other institutes (eg. Spartan).

We understand that embarking on a new project or technology can be intimidating, and our team is here to help you design and implement your workloads. If you're starting a new project and unsure about the most suitable compute options, don't hesitate to contact us at research.computing@wehi.edu.au. Our team is ready to assist you and provide guidance to ensure the successful execution of your research projects.

Storage 

For details about the storage options, please visit make sure you thoroughly read this page before you get started with data storage on Milton. There are storage options on our high performance storage (VAST) for individual scratch space and project space.   We recommend the use of all Stornext shares for archiving data only. All active processing of data should be done on VAST. 

Understanding file permissions is essential to any user of Milton. Sharing files is very useful but make sure you share your files and directories in a safe way and with only the intended user(s). Please make sure you read this guide before you start sharing files and directories.  

To get access to your lab share, please email a request to support@wehi.edu.au.
