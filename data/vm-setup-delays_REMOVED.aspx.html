VM (virtual machine) set up delays

For detailed instructions on how to provision a VM, read the Provisioning VMs page on this website.

This page provides instructions on what to do if you encounter a delay when requesting a VM via vra.wehi.edu.au/vcac/org/Milton.

Instructions

The carousel below contains the instructions to follow if you encounter a delay when requesting a VM via vra.wehi.edu.au/vcac/org/Milton.

Scroll through the instructions by clicking the arrow on the middle right-hand side of each image. It is also possible to go back by clicking the opposite arrow on the left-hand side of each image.

(The final 3 instructions are text only without images so they are featured as the final image in the carousel, as well as detailed beneath the carousel in case you prefer to read text on screen and not as a screenshot.)

(The instructions in this carousel are also detailed below for printing & searching processes)​​​​​​​

1. From the VM home page request one of the servers that you need to perform your work.

2a. Click the 'request' link and complete the online form.

2b. Complete the form and click the 'submit' button.

3. The 'Deployments' screen will appear and displays a blue bar indicating the progress next to the VM name.

4. A percentage bar will appear, however, please note that the percentage bar is only a guideline of how much time is required to provision your chosen VM, and is not verbatim.

5. In general a request should be fulfilled within a short space of time (less than 30mins), however, there are factors that can affect the time it takes for a job to complete, eg. high workload, multiple requests, system trying to find available resources, etc.

6. Occasionally your request may get greyed out and say ‘pending approval’. Don’t worry if this happens, it just means the system is finding resources for you and your request is in progress.

Recommendation

The Research Computing Team would like to recommend that when a request to provision a VM is submitted, please be patient and give the system some time to complete your job.

Most requests are usually fulfilled within a 40-minute timeframe but if your request takes longer to complete, please contact the Helpdesk so they can investigate further.
