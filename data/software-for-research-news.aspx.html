Software used for research purposes

Software is an essential part of research and has been for a long time. WEHI researchers use scientific software everyday and often develop their own software. 

The importance of software for WEHI research can be observed from WEHI Nature publications in the figure in the right-hand column. Note that for 2021, the number is only up to mid-July.

The Research Computing Platform (RCP) can collaborate with you and your lab on software engineering aspects of your research and help you convert your ideas into tools. RCP provides a range of services from automating repetitive processes, building tools, modifying open source software on which you rely, making early research tools available to a wide audience.

For more ambitious projects we can help specify and design the solution and co-supervise the software engineers who build it. We can also recruit and co-supervise student interns.

All of the RCP team have significant research engineering experience in our backgrounds so, if you have thought, "I wish there was a bit of software to do such and such" or "I wish this bit of software had this feature", please get in touch with Research Computing for a chat.​​​​​​​​​​​​​​
